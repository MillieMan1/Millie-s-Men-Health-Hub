Fitness

Excercise is very important to your health because it makes you swole. Swole is the goal, size is the prize Milly, you know what I'm talking about. But seriously, staying in tip-top shape will help reduce chances of problems such as obesity, diabetes, and heart problems. As much as it may stress you out just to think about exercising, once you actually start working out, you'll experience less stress in every part of your life. And when you improve your strength and stamina, it's easier to accomplish everyday tasks like carrying groceries and climbing stairs. This also helps you feel more energetic over the course of the day.
